Project Hoplomancer

(2023 - Present)

An in-development fast-paced arcade FPS built on Unreal Engine 5. Set in a dark cyberpunk world, fight in an arena and survive an endless onslaught of increasingly difficult enemies. Try to get a high score!
I am responsible for its programming, game design, level design, graphic design, most 3D models & textures, art, writing, etc.

INERTIA

(2019 - Present)

An in-development semi-total-conversion modification of Half-Life 2: Episode 2. I am the lead developer and primarily responsible for its programming, game design, level design, graphic design, 3D models, textures, art, writing, etc.
I've implemented numerous additions & modifications to the baseline gameplay, including:

  • New & reprogrammed weapons
  • Inventory system
  • Modified AI
  • Encumbrance system
  • Dynamic crosshair system
  • Usable items
  • New NPCs
  • Numerous bug fixes

You can find more information about the project on ModDB.
